I am a photographer / videographer currently living in Portland, Oregon. Originally from Salt Lake City, Utah, I lived in Austin, Texas for a short stint in 2016, before moving to the great Northwest in 2017. I've been working with my camera for the better part of the last decade and am well versed with the Adobe creative cloud, specifically Premiere, After Effects, Photoshop and Lightroom. I love photography, but I feel that the future is in technology and I don't want to be left behind. With this belief in mind, I have dedicated all of my time and effort to learning HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL and C# with the .NET framework. I know that I have a long road ahead, but I'm well on my way and I'm excited to keep learning and growing as a programmer.
Thank you for taking the time to check out my website and please, feel free to reach out with any questions!
